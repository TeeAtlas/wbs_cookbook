Ingridients
100ml olive oil, plus extra for greasing
250g asparagus spears, each cut into 3 pieces
200g self-raising flour
1 tbsp thyme leaves
100g sundried tomatoes, roughly chopped

Step 1

Heat oven to 190C/fan 170C/gas 5. Oil and line the base of a loaf tin(approx 22 x 10 x 5cm) with baking paper. Cook the asparagus in boiling, salted water for 2 mins, drain, then cool quickly under cold running water. Pat dry.

Step 2

Mix the flour and thyme with seasoning in a large bowl. Make a well in the center, then add the eggs, milk and oil, stirring all the time to draw the flour into the center. Beat for 1min to make a smooth batter.

Step 3

Reserve 5 asparagus tips and a few olive. Add the remaining asparagus, tomatoes, olieves and two-thirds of the cheese to the batter, then stir well. Spoon into the tin, scatter with the remaining cheese, then arrange the reserved asparagus and olieves on top. Sprinkle with the remaining cheese. Bake for 35-40 mins until the cake feels firm to the touch and is golden and crusty on top. Cool in the tin for 5 mins, then turn out.
